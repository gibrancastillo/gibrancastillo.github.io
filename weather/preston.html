Featured article: Family Activities

In the beautiful town of Preston, Idaho families enjoy exploring the beautiful scenery by hiking through the mountains, fishing in nearby rivers, and watching the sun set over the plains. Each season has different activities to offer. In the winter months, families change into their snow gear to ski, snowshoe, or snowmobile. If staying warm is more your style, then there are wonderful resorts with hot tubs and indoor activities that provide for ample family time and creating memories to last a lifetime.
