Target Audience

The target audience of the blueskyweather.info web site is described below:

  • Who: It can be anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. It can be used by anyone who has access to the site.
  • Age: 12 to 100
  • Motivation: Provide information people and organizations can use to reduce weather-related losses and enhance societal benefits, including protection of life and property, public health and safety, and support of economic prosperity and quality of life.
